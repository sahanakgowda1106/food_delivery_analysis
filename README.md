# 📊 Food Delivery Data Analysis (Python Project)

## 🚀 Overview
This project performs an end-to-end data analysis of a food delivery dataset to uncover insights into customer behavior, delivery efficiency, and business performance.

The project demonstrates real-world data analysis using Python, including data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and visualization.

## 🎯 Objectives
- Perform data cleaning and preprocessing  
- Conduct exploratory data analysis (EDA)  
- Analyze delivery performance and customer satisfaction  
- Evaluate revenue trends across cities and restaurants  
- Generate actionable business insights  

---

## 📁 Dataset Description
- Contains 1200+ records

### Features:
- Order_ID  
- Customer_ID  
- Order_Date  
- City  
- Restaurant_Name  
- Food_Category  
- Item_Name  
- Quantity  
- Order_Value  
- Delivery_Time_Min  
- Payment_Method  
- Rating  

---

## 🧹 Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
- Handled missing values in Rating  
- Removed duplicate records  
- Standardized city names, restaurant names, and payment methods  
- Converted Order_Date to datetime format  
- Created new column: Delivery_Status (Fast / Normal / Late)  

---

## 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

### Analysis Performed:
- Orders distribution by city  
- Revenue by city and restaurant  
- Most popular food items and categories  
- Monthly and daily trends  
- Payment method analysis  
- Delivery time distribution  
- Relationship between delivery time and ratings  

---

## 📈 Visualizations
- Bar charts  
- Line plots  
- Pie charts  
- Scatter plots  
- Boxplots  
- Heatmaps  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Bangalore generates the highest revenue  
- Fast Food and Biryani are the most popular categories  
- UPI is the most used payment method  
- Faster deliveries result in higher customer ratings  
- A small number of restaurants contribute to most of the revenue  

---

## 📌 Business Recommendations
- Improve delivery speed to increase customer satisfaction  
- Focus on high-performing cities and restaurants  
- Introduce loyalty programs for frequent customers  
- Optimize operations during peak demand periods
